# Tourism in Turkey

This project is about tourism in Turkey and highlights some of the country's most famous and beautiful destinations. Turkey is known for its rich history, natural beauty, and unique culture that attracts millions of visitors every year.

## Featured Places
- **Istanbul** – Famous for Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, and Bosphorus views.  
- **Cappadocia** – Known for hot air balloons and unique rock formations.  
- **Antalya** – A beautiful coastal city with beaches and resorts.  
- **Pamukkale** – Famous for white thermal terraces and natural hot springs.  
- **Ephesus** – One of the most important ancient cities in the world.  

## Project Features
- Information about top tourist attractions  
- Easy and simple design  
- Educational travel guide
