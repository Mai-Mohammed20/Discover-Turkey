Fethiye is one of the most beautiful coastal destinations in Turkey.
It is located on the Mediterranean Sea and is famous for its crystal-clear water and stunning natural landscapes.
The city is well known for Ölüdeniz,
a breathtaking blue lagoon that attracts tourists from all over the world. Visitors can enjoy swimming, boat trips, and even paragliding from Babadağ Mountain.
Fethiye also has ancient ruins and traditional markets,
which give visitors a chance to explore Turkish culture and history.
